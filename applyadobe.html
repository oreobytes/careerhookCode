  • Job Description

    We are an advanced technology and design group working on the future of collaboration at Adobe. Our team is building a v1 product focused on exploring, testing, and demonstrating how we can improve the creative process for everyone involved in it – from artists and designers to reviewers and builders, and everyone else along the way. We are looking for a holistic designer who can thrive in an experimental, open-ended, and fast-moving problem space. Qualified candidates should have an excellent understanding of UX/UI design principles, a love for critical analysis and problem solving, and a strong ability to articulate the rationale behind their design decisions. Our ideal candidate is curious, self-motivated, and passionate about asking the right questions. You’ll be collaborating with a small team of passionate designers and engineers to bring this work to life, and to power the next generation of Adobe’s creative products.

  • Requirements

  • Demonstrated understanding of fundamental visual & interaction design principles
  • Portfolio showcasing your best work
  • Proficient with prototyping tools such as Adobe XD, Sketch, InVision, etc.
  • Working knowledge of UX research practices
  • Strong interpersonal, written and oral communication skills.
  • Enjoy working in a highly-collaborative environment but are equally comfortable working independently and owning your designs from start to finish
  • A self-starter with an ability to learn quickly

  • Summary of Requirements

  • Work with multiple stakeholders, including product managers, engineers, and other designers to draft initial concepts.
  • Analyze user pain points, business goals, research findings and translate them to beautiful and functional design solutions.
  • Produce UX deliverables to support project goals. This can include user journey maps, interaction flows, wireframes, prototypes, high-fidelity mockups, animations, etc.
  • Support development teams throughout the implementation cycle
  • Work with other design teams within Adobe to ensure that our product fits into the Adobe ecosystem.
  • Be proactive about gathering feedback on your designs early and often.
  • Be an advocate for Adobe’s customers
