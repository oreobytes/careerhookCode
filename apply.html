    User Experience/User Interface (UI/UX) expert responsible for supporting both consumer-facing and internal-facing digital environments. Conducts user research, interviews, and surveys; translates research findings and business requirements into sitemaps, wireframes and prototypes. Builds dev-ready compelling designs and interactive user interfaces that meet functional needs and bring the brand to life.

  • Description of Duties

  • Work cross functionally across NOA Consumer Service and with business managers to define a vision for the customer support, ecommerce, and agent experiences and develop a roadmap of incremental improvements
  • Perform foundational UX activities, including: user testing, card sorting, tree stumps, personas, usability studies and research, creating and implementing surveys for direct customer feedback, analyzing usage data and traffic patterns, conducting A/B tests to measure customer response
  • Perform foundational UI design activities, including: translate wireframes into prototypes and design mockups that leverage a user-centered design approach, conform to style guides, and implement thoughtful interaction states, produce dev-ready assets
  • Make deliberate design decisions to translate design concepts and business requirements into user stories, user journeys, storyboards, process flows, and wireframes that lead to intuitive user experiences optimized for a wide range of devices and interfaces
  • Perform competitive research and analysis to include in design recommendations
  • Create wireframes and clickable prototypes with a variety of design and prototyping tools such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Fireworks, Dreamweaver, Visio, Axure, Omnigraffle, etc.
  • Present design concepts and deliverables to stakeholders
  • Identify design problems and devise elegant solutions
  • Make strategic design and user-experience decisions related to core and new functions and features and rapidly test and iterate designs
  • Execute all visual design stages from concept to final hand-off to engineering including defining and maintaining visual style guides and developing assets
  • Keep abreast of the latest changes in industry standards

  • Summary of Requirements

  • Minimum of three years of experience and proven track record of UX strategy leading to UI project implementation
  • Strong portfolio of both UX and UI skill sets
  • Excellent visual design skills with sensitivity to user-system interaction and brand guidelines
  • Hands-on experience creating wireframes, prototypes, storyboards, user flows, etc.
  • Experience using tools such as Photoshop, Sketch, Illustrator or other visual design and wire-framing tools
  • Understanding of basic front-end languages: HTML5, CSS3, Javascript
  • Experience in an ecommerce or service support environment preferred
  • Experience working in an agile/scrum development process
  • Up-to-date with the latest UI trends, techniques, and technologies
  • BS/BA in Human-Computer Interaction, Interaction Design, or equivalent M.S./M.A. degree a plus
